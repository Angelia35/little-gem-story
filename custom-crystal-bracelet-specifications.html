Quick answer

Fully custom bracelets generally start around US$60, include up to two reasonable revisions before production, require a 30% deposit after quote confirmation and usually take about 5–14 days to produce. Ready-to-ship pieces are individually priced and may be lower or higher. Customers are encouraged to send a budget first so Auralis can recommend the most suitable route.

Design Inputs

Parameters Auralis uses before recommending a route

Customers do not need to know every stone name. These inputs are enough to begin a useful recommendation.

BudgetRequired decision input

Helps determine whether ready-to-ship, simple personalization or full custom is realistic.

Wrist sizeExact, estimated or not sure

Exact circumference is preferred. Fit preference and bead size are confirmed together.

Color directionBlue, clear, warm, green, pink, mixed

A color mood can be more useful than guessing a stone name.

PurposeDaily wear, gift or symbolic design

Use and recipient context affect style, bead scale and packaging direction.

Reference photoOptional but useful

Helps clarify layout, contrast, bead scale and accessory style.

Delivery countryNeeded for final logistics

Shipping timing and cost are confirmed separately after destination review.
