What it means

Birth Date, MBTI and Five Elements Work as Style Signals

Customers can use birth date to create a simple element direction, MBTI to express personality tone, and crystal families to guide color and stone mood.

This keeps the page interactive and personalized without making customers fill a long form before they are ready to inquire.

Important note

Designed as Jewelry, Not a Promise

The result is a symbolic design suggestion only. We avoid medical claims, guaranteed luck, destiny-changing language or therapeutic promises.

Recommended wording: “element-inspired”, “symbolic”, “personal meaning”, “color direction”, “meaningful gift”.
No need to know exact stones

Customers Can Simply Share a Feeling

Many customers may not know which stone to choose. The Element Style Finder gives them a simple design brief they can copy into the custom request form.

The final bracelet is still confirmed manually based on material availability, design feasibility and shipping requirements.

Tip: customers do not need to know every crystal name in advance. They can simply submit a direction like “Water + calm + blue tones” or “Fire + love + warm colors”, and the final design can be built from there.
