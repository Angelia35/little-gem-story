FAQ

Questions before choosing a bracelet gift

How do I choose a crystal bracelet gift if I do not know her favorite stone?

Start with her favorite colors, clothing style, the gift occasion, approximate wrist size and your budget. Auralis can recommend suitable available or custom directions.

Can I request a gift below US$60?

Some ready-to-ship or existing designs may be below US$60, while others may be above it. Fully custom requests generally start around US$60. Share the budget first so Auralis can check suitable options.

What if I do not know her wrist size?

Provide an existing bracelet measurement, an estimate or say that the size is unknown. The sizing direction can be discussed before production.

Can a gift be based on a birth date or Chinese element?

Yes. A birth date or Chinese element direction can be used as symbolic design inspiration. A standalone Five Elements reading is available for US$13.50.

Do crystal meanings guarantee an effect?

No. Meanings are symbolic and cultural and are used for personal reflection and gift storytelling, not guaranteed medical, financial or spiritual outcomes.
