Repeatable Case Structure

Fields required for each anonymous case

Anonymous case ID and customer typeExample format only: AJ-CASE-001 · Personal / Gift / Boutique. Do not show a name, email, exact address or account handle.
Original requestSummarize the real buyer goal, preferred colors, style, occasion and reference photo direction.
Budget bandUse a privacy-safe range, such as US$60–100, when exact price disclosure is not appropriate.
Wrist and bead parametersRecord actual wrist measurement, fit preference, approximate bead size and any sizing uncertainty.
Material direction and disclosureRecord proposed material names, treatment status if known, natural variation and accessory materials.
Recommended order routeReady-to-ship, simple personalization or fully custom, with the reason for the recommendation.
Revision recordDocument the actual changes requested and the final number of reasonable revision rounds.
TimelineRecord confirmed deposit date, production start, completion and shipment dates.
Final resultDescribe only what the records and finished-piece evidence support. Do not add an outcome claim without evidence.
