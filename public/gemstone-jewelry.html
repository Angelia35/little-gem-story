What to include in your request

  • Product type: ring, pendant or mixed selection
  • Gemstone name or reference image
  • Main stone size or approximate size
  • 925 silver, plated finish or other material request
  • Quantity, budget range and delivery country
  • Gift packaging or branding request if needed
