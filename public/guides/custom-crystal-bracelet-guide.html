A custom crystal bracelet can begin from a real reference photo, color mood, stone direction, wrist size or gift occasion. This guide uses real Auralis reference material, so the visual examples show actual uploaded bracelet styles instead of invented product designs.

Quick request rule: you do not need a perfect design brief. A clear photo, a color direction or a gift purpose is enough to begin.

1. Start with the style you already like

The easiest way to request a custom bracelet is to send a photo of a style you like. A real reference image helps confirm bead size, color tone, layout, charm direction and overall feeling.

2. Choose the color and wearing mood

Instead of inventing a new style, choose from the real material direction: blue clear tone, warm amber tone, pastel mixed color, yellow crystal tone or purple-blue clear tone.

  • Blue tone: calm, clean and refreshing.
  • Warm amber tone: mature, sunny and elegant.
  • Pastel mixed color: soft, playful and gift-friendly.
  • Purple-blue clear tone: dreamy, cool and luminous.
