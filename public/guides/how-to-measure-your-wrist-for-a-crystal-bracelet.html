Bracelet Size & Fit Guide

How to Measure Your Wrist for a Custom Crystal Bracelet

Send the exact wrist measurement—not only “small” or “medium.” Auralis can then confirm the fit allowance based on bead size, design and whether you prefer a close, comfortable or loose fit.

Quick answer

Wrap a soft tape directly around the wrist where the bracelet will sit. Keep it close without squeezing. Record the exact number and tell Auralis whether you prefer a close, comfortable or loose fit.

1. Measure the exact wrist circumference

1

Wrap

Place the tape around the wrist area where you normally wear a bracelet.

2

Do not squeeze

Keep the tape close to the skin without pulling it tight.

3

Record

Send the exact number in centimeters or inches.

2. Use string or paper when no tape is available

Wrap a non-stretch string, ribbon or paper strip around the wrist. Mark the point where it meets, lay it flat and measure the marked length with a ruler.

Do not use a highly elastic cord because it can stretch and produce an inaccurate result.

3. Choose the fit feeling

Fit preferenceHow it feelsWhat to send

Do not add the final allowance yourself unless requested. Bead size and construction affect inner circumference.

5. Measure for a gift

The safest option is to measure a bracelet the recipient already wears comfortably. Measure its inner circumference where possible, not only the outside diameter.

  • Tell Auralis that the order is a gift.
  • Share an existing bracelet measurement or estimated wrist size.
  • Explain whether the recipient usually prefers close or loose jewelry.
  • Confirm the final size before production.

6. Send sizing details with the request

FAQ

How do I measure my wrist for a crystal bracelet?

Wrap a soft measuring tape directly around the wrist where the bracelet will sit. Keep it close without squeezing and send the exact wrist measurement in centimeters or inches.

Should I add extra length myself?

It is better to send the exact wrist measurement and preferred fit. Final bracelet size also depends on bead diameter and construction, so Auralis can confirm the allowance before production.

How does bead size affect bracelet fit?

Larger beads take up more inner space and can feel tighter at the same overall circumference. The final bracelet size should therefore be confirmed together with bead size.

What if I do not know the wrist size for a gift?

Tell Auralis it is a gift and provide an estimate, an existing bracelet measurement or the recipient's general wrist size. The safest direction can be discussed before production.

Can I start the request before confirming size?

Yes. You can start with budget, reference photo, color mood, birth date, Chinese element direction or gift purpose and confirm wrist size later.
