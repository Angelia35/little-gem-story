How to Request Custom Jewelry Online Before Quote

You do not need a complete design file to request custom jewelry. A reference photo, stone preference, color mood, size, gift occasion or simple idea is enough to start the conversation before quote.

To request custom jewelry online, send the jewelry type, reference photo, stone or color preference, size, quantity, delivery country and gift occasion before quote. These details help confirm whether the design can be made and what direction fits your budget and style.

Important: you can send your idea before payment. The design direction, material options and quote are confirmed first.

1. Details to send first

A clear request does not need to be long. Send the essential details first, then confirm more details during the conversation.

Jewelry typeBracelet, ring, pendant, necklace, earrings or gemstone piece.
Reference photoA saved image, screenshot, drawing or similar style you like.
Stone or colorCrystal name, gemstone name, color mood or symbolic meaning.
Size and quantityWrist size, ring size, pendant size, one piece or multiple gifts.
Gift occasionBirthday, anniversary, friendship, holiday or personal meaning.
Delivery countryThe country helps estimate shipping direction and timing.

2. Can you send a reference photo?

Yes. Reference photos are helpful because they show the style faster than a long explanation. The final design may need adjustment based on available stones, structure and production feasibility.

Example request:
I want a custom crystal bracelet similar to this photo. I like soft pink and clear stones. It is a birthday gift for my friend. Wrist size is around 16 cm. Please help check if this style can be quoted.

3. How the request process works

1Send idea
Send photo, product type, color, stone or gift purpose.
2Confirm direction
We review style, materials and design feasibility.
3Receive quote
Quote is shared after key details are confirmed.
4Confirm order
Payment and production happen after details are agreed.

4. No payment before quote

For a custom request, you can ask first. We confirm the design direction, stone options and quote before payment. This lowers the risk of sending an idea when you are not sure what is possible.

5. What makes a good custom request?

A good custom jewelry request is specific enough to understand, but it does not need to be perfect. These are useful examples:

  • I want a rose quartz bracelet gift for a birthday.
  • I have a reference photo and want a similar gemstone pendant.
  • I need a purple crystal bracelet with a calm and elegant feeling.
  • I want a custom jewelry gift, but I only know her favorite color is green.
