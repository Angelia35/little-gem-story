Quick answer

Blue & Clear tones are useful when the desired bracelet should feel calm, clean and refreshing. Send this page or another reference photo with a budget, wrist size and gift purpose so Auralis can recommend a suitable available or custom direction.

1. What this color direction communicates

The page describes color and visual mood rather than promising one exact mineral. The final material direction depends on availability, budget, bead size and the level of personalization.

Tell Auralis the budget first. Depending on current availability and the requested changes, Auralis may recommend ready-to-ship, simple personalization or a fully custom design. No payment is required before the direction, price and estimated timeline are confirmed.
