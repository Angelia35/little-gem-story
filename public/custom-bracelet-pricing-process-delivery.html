FAQ

Pricing and process questions

What should I send before asking for a bracelet price?

Send your preferred budget, color or style direction, wrist size, whether it is for yourself or a gift, delivery country and any reference photo. Auralis can then recommend a suitable route.

What is the difference between ready-to-ship and fully custom?

Ready-to-ship uses a completed design with limited availability and no custom design revisions. Fully custom is developed around your request and includes up to two reasonable revisions before production.

Is the US$60 amount a fixed price?

No. It is a general starting point for fully custom bracelet requests. Final pricing depends on materials, bead size, complexity, accessories, packaging and other custom details.

When is the 30% deposit paid?

The deposit is requested only after the design direction, quote and estimated timeline are confirmed. It begins material preparation and production.

Can I cancel after paying the deposit?

Contact Auralis immediately. If material preparation or production has started, the custom deposit is non-refundable because it covers design time, materials and made-to-order work.

What counts as two reasonable revisions?

Reasonable revisions include color balance, bead arrangement, available accessories or minor details within the confirmed direction. A complete redesign, major material replacement or additional design rounds may require a revised quote.

Is shipping included in the bracelet price?

Shipping cost and estimated delivery time are confirmed separately based on destination, package details and the selected shipping method.

What happens if the item arrives damaged or incorrect?

Contact Auralis within 30 days of delivery with clear photos and order details. The appropriate solution may include adjustment guidance, repair, replacement parts, remake or another agreed resolution depending on the issue.
