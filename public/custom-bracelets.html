Ways to customize your crystal bracelet

Each request can start from a different point. Choose the one that feels easiest.

Customize by reference photo

Send a bracelet photo, screenshot or style you like. It helps us understand color, bead size, bracelet structure and overall mood.

I like this reference style. I prefer [color mood]. My wrist size is [size]. Please check if this can be quoted.

Customize by birth date or Chinese element

Send your birth date or preferred element as a symbolic design direction. We can suggest a color mood before quote.

FAQ

Custom crystal bracelet questions

Can I customize a crystal bracelet from a reference photo?

Yes. A photo or screenshot is enough to start. We use it to understand the style direction before quote.

Do I need to know the exact stone name?

No. You can simply describe your preferred color mood, style, birth date, element or gift purpose.

Can I request a birth date or Chinese element bracelet?

Yes. Birth date and Chinese element-inspired requests are accepted as symbolic design directions.

Do I pay before the quote?

No. We confirm the design direction, material options, quote and timing before payment.

Can I order only one bracelet?

You can send the request first. Quantity, delivery country and available options will be confirmed before quote.

Can this be a gift order?

Yes. Tell us the gift occasion, preferred color and any message or meaning you want the bracelet to carry.
