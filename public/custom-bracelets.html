FAQ

Questions before requesting a bracelet

How much does a fully custom crystal bracelet cost?

Fully custom-made bracelet requests generally start around US$60 and can reach several hundred dollars. The quote depends on materials, bead size, accessories, design complexity, packaging and other personalization details.

Can Auralis recommend a bracelet based on my budget?

Yes. Send your preferred budget, color or style direction, wrist size, gift purpose and delivery country. Auralis can review ready-to-ship, simple personalization and fully custom directions before quoting.

Are ready-to-ship bracelets always cheaper than custom bracelets?

No. Ready-to-ship bracelets are priced individually. Some may be below US$60, while others may be above US$60 depending on materials, bead size, accessories and craftsmanship.

How many revisions are included with a fully custom bracelet?

Up to two reasonable revisions are included before production. These can cover color balance, bead arrangement, available accessories and minor details within the confirmed design direction.

Do I pay before receiving a quote?

No. Auralis reviews the request and confirms the design direction, available options, price and estimated timeline before payment.

How long does a custom bracelet take?

Typical production is approximately 5 to 14 days after the design direction, quote and 30% deposit are confirmed. Complex designs or special materials may require additional time.

What is the Chinese Five Elements reading?

A standalone Five Elements reading is available for US$13.50. It can cover the primary element profile, stronger or weaker elements, elements that may be missing, general current-year themes, areas to be mindful of and answers to one or two questions. It is offered for cultural interest and personal reflection.
