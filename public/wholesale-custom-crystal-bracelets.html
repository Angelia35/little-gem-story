Material Trust

Wholesale descriptions should be clear before products are sold

Review material wording before approving the assortment

Auralis uses a disclosure-first approach for known material direction, treatments, laboratory-grown or imitation materials, natural variation, bead size and metal components. Wholesale buyers should also use accurate descriptions in product listings and customer-facing materials.

Wholesale FAQ

Questions from boutiques and retailers

Who is the wholesale service for?

Boutiques, gift shops, online stores, jewelry retailers and other small businesses can submit a wholesale inquiry. Auralis reviews the business type, target market, target retail price, budget, quantity and style direction before recommending options.

Is there one fixed wholesale MOQ?

No single MOQ is stated for every project. Minimum quantity depends on the selected design, material availability, packaging, branding and customization level. MOQ is confirmed in the quotation.

Can I start with a trial order or sample?

Ask in the wholesale inquiry. Sample availability, sample cost, trial-order quantity and whether the sample can be credited to a later order are confirmed separately for the selected project.

Can I mix different bracelet styles?

Mixed styles may be possible for some ready-to-ship or selected collections, but eligibility depends on availability, total quantity and packaging requirements. It is not guaranteed until confirmed.

Can Auralis add my logo or custom packaging?

Brand cards, labels, message cards or packaging may be available depending on quantity, artwork, material and budget. Setup cost, MOQ and lead time are quoted before production.

How should I request a wholesale recommendation?

Send your target retail price, initial purchase budget, estimated quantity, preferred styles, packaging requirements, target market and delivery country. Auralis will then recommend a suitable ready-to-ship, simply personalized or custom capsule direction.

How long does a wholesale order take?

Lead time depends on quantity, materials, sampling, packaging and customization. A specific production and shipping estimate is confirmed with the quotation.

Are wholesale material descriptions handled differently?

The same disclosure principles apply. Known material direction, treatments, uncertainty, bead size, metal parts and care information should be confirmed for the selected assortment before production.
