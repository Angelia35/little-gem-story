FAQ

Wholesale inquiry form questions

What should I enter for target retail price?

Enter the approximate price you expect customers to pay for one bracelet in your market. This helps Auralis recommend material and design directions that may fit your positioning.

Can I select “Let Auralis recommend”?

Yes. It is the preferred option when you are open to ready-to-ship, selected adjustments or a custom capsule collection.

Does submitting the form create an order?

No. The form creates an inquiry message only. MOQ, sample terms, quotation, production time, payment and shipping are confirmed separately before any order proceeds.

Can I attach product references?

Add a public link in the form or attach images after WhatsApp opens. You can also email references to hello@auralisgems.com.
