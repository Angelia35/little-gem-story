FAQ

Questions about the request form

Do I need to choose ready-to-ship or custom before submitting?

No. Select Let Auralis recommend and share your budget and preferences. Auralis can check available and custom directions.

What if I do not know my wrist size?

Enter Not sure yet. You can use the wrist measurement guide or confirm sizing before production.

Can I attach a reference photo?

The budget request builder creates a WhatsApp or email message. After WhatsApp opens, attach your image directly in the chat. You can also include a reference link in the form.

When will I be asked to pay?

No payment is requested before Auralis reviews the request and confirms the direction, quote, timing and relevant order terms.

Can I request only a Five Elements reading?

Yes. The standalone reading is US$13.50 and can be requested without ordering a bracelet.
