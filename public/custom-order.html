What to prepare

You do not need complete specifications. A simple idea, reference image or product direction is enough to start.

  • Product type: bracelet, ring, pendant, set or gift order
  • Birth date or preferred Chinese element if you want an element-inspired bracelet
  • Preferred color mood, symbolic meaning, stone direction or reference style
  • Wrist size, quantity, delivery country, budget range or deadline if known
  • Gift occasion, packaging, message card or branding requirements
  • Any reference photos, links or screenshots you want us to review

How payment works

There is no payment before your request is reviewed. We confirm the key details first, then send quote and next steps.

  • Submit request first
  • We review style, material and availability
  • Quote is confirmed before payment
  • Production or sourcing starts after confirmation

What happens next?

The process is designed to make custom jewelry requests feel simple, low-pressure and clear.

1

We review your request

2

We may ask follow-up questions about wrist size, color, material, gift purpose, packaging or budget.

3

You receive a quote

We send available options, estimated cost and timeline before payment.

4

Order is arranged

After confirmation, we arrange production, sourcing, packaging and shipping.
