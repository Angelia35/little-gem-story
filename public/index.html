FAQ

Questions before you send a custom request

How does a custom crystal bracelet order work?

You can start by sending a reference photo, birth date, Chinese element request, color mood, wrist size or gift idea. We review your request and confirm the design direction, material options, quote and timeline before payment.

Can I send only a reference photo?

Yes. A photo or screenshot is enough to begin. You can also add preferred colors, wrist size, delivery country or gift purpose to help us understand the request faster.

Do I need to pay before the quote is confirmed?

No. Payment is not required before the design direction, material options, quote and timeline are confirmed.

Are the crystals natural?

Auralis offers natural crystal and gemstone-inspired material options. Material details can be discussed before quote confirmation. Natural stones may vary in color, pattern and texture.

What if I am not sure about my wrist size?

You can tell us you are not sure yet. We will help confirm sizing guidance before production.

How long does custom jewelry take?

Timeline depends on design complexity, material availability, quantity and destination country. We confirm the estimated crafting and shipping timeline before the order moves forward.
