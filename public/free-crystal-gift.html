FAQ

Questions before claiming the Mystery Crystal Gift

Is the Auralis Mystery Crystal Gift really free?

The mystery crystal gift itself is free for new visitors during the offer period. The customer covers the shipping fee. One gift is available per new customer, and gift style depends on available stock.

Why do I need to cover shipping?

The shipping fee helps cover international delivery and handling for the free gift. The gift itself is not charged, and custom bracelet orders are quoted separately.

Can I choose the exact crystal gift?

The mystery gift is selected based on available stock and the preferred color direction you share. Exact stone type and final gift style are not guaranteed for the free gift offer.

Can I ask about a custom crystal bracelet at the same time?

Yes. You can claim the mystery crystal gift and also ask about a custom bracelet by sending a reference photo, birth date, Chinese element request, color mood, wrist size or gift purpose.

Do I need to pay for a custom bracelet now?

No. Payment is not required before the custom bracelet design direction, material options, quote and timeline are confirmed.

How long will the gift take to ship?

Shipping time depends on the destination country and available delivery options. We confirm shipping details after receiving your claim request.
