# Auralis 批发询盘 Tally 字段方案

如果后续希望把 `/wholesale-inquiry` 改成 Tally 后台收集，建议单独新建批发表单，不要和个人 Custom Order 混用。

## 必填字段
1. Business / Store Name
2. Business Type
3. Target Market / Delivery Country
4. Target Retail Price per Piece
5. Preferred Initial Purchase Budget
6. Estimated Initial Quantity
7. Preferred Styles / Colors / Reference Direction
8. Contact Name
9. Email

## 选填字段
- Website / Social Page
- Order Direction: Let Auralis Recommend / Ready-to-Ship / Simple Personalization / Custom Capsule
- Packaging / Labels / Branding
- Target Launch Date
- WhatsApp
- Reference Image Upload
- Other Requirements

## 提交成功提示
Thank you. Auralis has received your boutique wholesale inquiry. We will review your target retail price, budget, quantity, style and packaging direction before confirming suitable options, MOQ, sample terms, price and lead time. No payment is required before quote confirmation.
